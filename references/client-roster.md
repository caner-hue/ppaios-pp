## How to read this

Each client has:
- **Status** — active, paused, completed, lead, churned
- **Tier** — owned (PP-owned brand), retainer (monthly fee), sprint (fixed-scope project), commission (paid on results), audit (one-off)
- **Engagement window** — start/end + duration
- **Scope** — what PP is delivering
- **Invoicing** — fee, cadence, channel, last invoice date
- **Stakeholders** — primary client contact + PP-side lead
- **Tools** — Shopify URL, Klaviyo account, ad accounts, etc.
- **Open questions** — what's blocked or undecided
